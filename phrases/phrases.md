## Asking about Time and Dates

These are the most common questions for asking about the time, day, or date, utilizing the vocabulary and rules from the Time and Dates notes.

| English | Romaji | Japanese |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **What time is it now?** | Ima nan-ji desu ka? | 今は何時ですか。 |
| **What day is it today?** | Kyou wa nan-nichi desu ka? | 今日は何日ですか。 |
| **What day of the week is it?** | Kyou wa nan-youbi desu ka? | 今日は何曜日ですか。 |
| **What month is it?** | Ima nan-gatsu desu ka? | 今は何月ですか。 |
| **When is your birthday?** | Tanjoubi wa itsu desu ka? | 誕生日はいつですか。 |

> [!TIP]
> Notice the pattern! Japanese heavily relies on the question word **何 (nan / nani)** meaning "what". 
> - What time = **nan-ji** (何時)
> - What day = **nan-nichi** (何日)
> - What month = **nan-gatsu** (何月)
> 
> The word for "when" is **itsu** (いつ).
